Sartoria Panettieri

About Us

Welcome to Sartoria Panettieri, San Sebastian's premier pizzeria where tradition meets local flavor. Since 2018, our family-run restaurant has crafted delicious pizzas with the finest local produce and our signature sourdough crust.

Located in the heart of Basque Country, we blend time-honored recipes with fresh, high-quality ingredients from nearby farms. Experience the warmth of our family, the richness of our community, and the taste of our handcrafted pizzas. Join us for a meal that celebrates local flavor fused with Italian recipes passed down through generations

Taste

We invite you to explore a menu that showcases our local culinary traditions. Our pizzas, made with a unique sourdough crust, feature ingredients sourced from the lush farms around San Sebastian, selected based on the season.

To complement your meal, we offer natural wines from nearby vineyards, chosen to enhance our dishes. Experience the harmony of locally-sourced produce and exceptional winemaking, crafted with care and dedication. Enjoy a true gastronomic taste of Basque Country with every bite and sip.
