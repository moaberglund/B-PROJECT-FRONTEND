Sartoria Panettieri

About Us

Welcome to Sartoria Panettieri, San Sebastian's premier pizzeria where tradition meets local flavor.
Since opening our doors in 2018, our family-run establishment has been dedicated to crafting delicious pizzas using the finest local produce and our signature sourdough crust.

Nestled in San Sebastian, the heart of Basque Country, we combine time-honored recipes with fresh, high-quality ingredients sourced from nearby farms.
At Sartoria Panettieri, we invite you to experience the warmth of our family, the richness of our community, and the unparalleled taste of our handcrafted pizzas.
Join us for a meal that celebrates local flavour fusioned with Italian recipes passed down through generations.

Taste

We invite you to explore a menu that highlights the best of our local culinary traditions.
Our pizzas, made with our unique sourdough crust, are adorned with ingredients sourced directly from the lush farms surrounding San Sebastian, carefully selected based on the season's bounty.

To complement your meal, we offer a carefully selected assortment of natural wines from nearby vineyards, each chosen to enhance the flavors of our dishes. Experience the perfect harmony of locally-sourced produce and exceptional winemaking, all crafted with the utmost care and dedication.
Enjoy a true gastronomic taste, farmed in Basque Country and brought to you with every bite and sip.
